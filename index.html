Nick Soper

ME, ME, ME

Hello, my name is Nick. I’ve been a professional geek for 13 years.

I was lucky enough to be born and raised in Bristol - one of the UK’s great cities. I had a great childhood with brilliant parents. I went to a really good school where I did well academically and had a decent group of friends - some of whom, I still count as my best friends today.

I carried on doing well - academically and socially - at college and university, and in 2005 I started my career as a professional geek.

Being a techie from Bristol, I didn’t have to look far for decent career opportunities. I’ve had four jobs in my career, and I’ve always moved to the next job as a positive, conscious choice - driven by a motivation to learn new skills and (more recently) to do some good in the world.

I like to think I have a good work ethic, I always want to go a great job, and I always want to make professional progress.

And in 2010, I even managed to trick someone into being my wife!
